How it works:
  1. Microphone mode (Mac): Play Teams audio through speakers. MeetPilot captures via your mic. Best if you're not wearing headphones.
  2. Screen Share mode (Windows): Select your entire screen, check "Share audio", and MeetPilot captures system audio directly.
  3. Screen Capture: If enabled, you'll be asked to share your screen — MeetPilot takes a screenshot every 30s so the AI can see slides, charts, and shared content being presented.
  4. Click Start Listening and allow access when prompted
  5. Suggestions appear automatically as the conversation builds, informed by both audio and visual context
