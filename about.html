About Vidhai

Where AI Takes Root — விதை

Our Story

Vidhai (விதை) means "seed" in Tamil — the beginning of something that grows.

Founded by Gowtham, a professional bridging manufacturing, quality engineering, and AI technology, Vidhai was created to be the resource he wished existed when navigating the rapidly evolving AI landscape.

The platform curates AI news, semiconductor industry insights, training resources, and practical AI tools — all through the lens of someone who understands both traditional industry operations and the transformative potential of artificial intelligence.

Our vision: Making AI knowledge accessible to professionals transitioning into the AI era, whether they come from manufacturing, engineering, business, or any other domain.
